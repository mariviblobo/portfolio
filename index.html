Development of Digital Permit To Work System
dic. 2020 - jul. 2021

Development and implementation of an internal web application for the company Cardon IV S. A., designed to digitally control and coordinate the process of application, approval, execution, and closing of work permits. This project contributed to the digital transformation of the company, as well as to the reduction of deviations in the permit issuance process; savings in the budget derived from the reduction of the use of paper; storage and backup of the generated data. In addition, with the digitalization of the process and the implementation of functions of generation of audits and statistics within the application, it was possible to reduce personnel contact during the process, thus reducing the risk of contagion of COVID-19.

Development of the Cardon IV Contract Management System (SACC)
ago. 2021 - feb. 2022

Development and implementation of an internal web application for the administration of contracts of the company Cardon IV S.A., which covers the entire contractual process from the adjudication to the contract closing. This project contributed to the digital transformation of the company and to improving compliance with the contract administration process, reducing the deviations identified in the audits.
This system facilitates contract management by having reminders and notifications via email about milestones or pending tasks. Likewise, it offers periodically updated information on the contracts through integration with SAP and allows the traceability of the actions carried out during the validity of the contracts.
